# Competitor Research & Market Intelligence Template
*(For use with Antigravity Step 0.5 — Pre-Build Research)*

## How to Use This Template
Before building any new portal or client site, copy this template into a new file
(e.g., `docs/research/CLIENT_NAME.md`) and ask Antigravity to fill it by scraping
the competitor's live site.

**Antigravity Command:**
```text
"Read the content from https://COMPETITOR-SITE.com/ and fill in the 
 Competitor Research template at docs/COMPETITOR_RESEARCH.md with your analysis."
```
